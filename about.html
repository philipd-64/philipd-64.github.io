About

Data analyst with expertise in Python, SQL, Tableau, and Excel, combining analytical skills with leadership experience and clear communication.

Data Analyst & Musician.

I'm Philip Davis, a recent data analyst based in Gig Harbor, Washington with a background in technical production management and music education. Over 14 years, I led tech teams at churches; managing everything from live production and recording of Sunday services to volunteer coaching. This allowed me develop my curiosity and create a strength for finding patterns in complex systems and making them work better for people.

That curiosity led me to complete CareerFoundry's Data Analytics Program with a specialization in Machine Learning. I've since built skills in Python, SQL, Tableau, and Excel through hands-on projects analyzing everything from video game sales trends to weather prediction models.

What I bring: Strong organizational skills, the ability to translate technical concepts for non-technical audiences, and experience leading teams through detail-oriented, high-pressure work. I combine analytical rigor with a people-first approach; the best insights mean nothing if they don't help real humans make better decisions.

What I'm looking for: Data analyst roles where I can combine technical analysis with clear communication and mission-driven work. I'm particularly drawn to organizations in music, nonprofits, or any field focused on making a meaningful impact. I'm open to full and part-time positions and remote work.

Beyond the data: When I'm not analyzing datasets, you'll find me playing bass, guitar, or keyboard, troubleshooting tech setups for friends and family, or diving into a new system to figure out how it ticks.
